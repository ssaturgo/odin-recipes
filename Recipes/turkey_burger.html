Ground Turkey Burger

Go Back

These juicy and healthy ground turkey burgers are pan-seared on a stovetop oven - no grill required! The patties are seasoned with pantry-staple ingredients, and seared on a cast iron skillet for a nice char. They’re quick and easy, and takes less than 30 minutes to make for a weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey - 1 lbs
  • Chopped Onions - 1/2 Cup
  • Soy Sauce - 1 Tbs
  • Garlic Powder - 1 Tsp
  • Ground Pepper - 1/2 Tsp
  • Cooking Oil - 2 Tbs
  • Hamburger Buns - 4
  • Tomato Slices
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese of Choice
  • Sauce of Choice

Instructions

  • 1) Add onion, soy sauce, garlic powder and pepper to the turkey meat and mix well.
  • 2) Form the turkey patties, about ½ inch thick, or a little bigger in diameter than your bun.
  • 3) Heat a cast iron skillet or a non-stick pan with cooking oil on medium to medium-high heat. When hot, cook the patties, about four mins on each side.
  • 4) Assemble your burger with your favorite condiments and toppings!
